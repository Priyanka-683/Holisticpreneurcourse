1. Introduction to Our Vision

At Holisticpreneur, we don't just sell courses; we facilitate human transformation. To do this effectively, we need to collect certain information. This policy governs all courses, workshops, and digital materials offered under the Holisticpreneur brand. By enrolling in any of our programs, you agree to the terms laid out in this document.

2. The Information We Protect

To provide a personalized learning experience, we collect information that falls into two categories:

  • Identity Information: This includes your full name, profile picture, and social media handles if you connect through them.
  • Learning Analytics: We track which modules you complete, the time spent on each lesson, and your quiz results to help you optimize your growth.

3. Course Access & Intellectual Property

When you purchase a course on Holisticpreneur, you are granted a single-user license. This means:

  • The content is for your personal evolution only. Sharing login credentials or redistributing course videos is strictly prohibited.
  • We use advanced digital watermarking to track unauthorized sharing. Piracy hurts the community and will result in permanent account termination without refund.
  • Course access duration (Lifetime vs. Limited) will be specified on the individual course checkout page.

4. Financial Terms & Refunds

All transactions are processed through secure, industry-leading encrypted gateways.

  • Refund Policy: Because our courses are digital and offer instant access to proprietary wisdom, we generally do not offer refunds once a module has been accessed. Please read the course curriculum carefully before enrolling.
  • Taxes: All course fees are inclusive or exclusive of GST/Taxes as mentioned at the time of checkout.

5. Security of Your Soul's Data

We treat your data with the same respect we treat our own. Your email address will never be sold to third-party advertisers. We only use your data to enhance your experience within the Holisticpreneur platform, such as sending you personalized course recommendations or invites to private masterclasses.

6. Global Compliance & Rights

Whether you are joining us from India, Europe, or the Americas, we aim to comply with the highest standards of data protection (including GDPR principles). You have the right to request a full export of your data or ask for its permanent deletion from our servers at any time.
